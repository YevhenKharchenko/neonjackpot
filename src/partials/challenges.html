TILE MATCHING & PUZZLE CHALLENGES

The core gameplay is basically Mahjong, but with some cool twists.

  • Match and combine tiles to clear the board

  • Master special mechanics like ice, grass, and gum tiles

  • Work your way through tons of levels (we spent ages designing each one)

  • Think ahead to avoid blocked paths

It gets harder as you go, but not in a frustrating way - more like a good challenge.
